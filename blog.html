How to Make Your Morning Purr-fect with Neko Pan's Signature Bakes

15 May 2025
Neko Pan Treats
Bakery Tips

Start your day with the warmth of freshly baked bread from Neko Pan! Whether you're a fan of sweet meringues or our signature sourdough, there's nothing like a morning pastry to satisfy your cravings.

At Neko Pan, we believe in the magic of slow baking, using traditional ingredients and traditional Japanese techniques to create treats that bring joy with every bite.

Here are a few ways to enjoy your morning with our baked delights:

  • Pair our fluffy croissants with matcha butter for a creamy twist
  • Warm up our anko-filled buns for a comforting start
  • Try our cat paw-shaped cookies with your morning coffee or tea

Our regular customers tell us their mornings aren't the same without a visit to Neko Pan, and we couldn't agree more!
