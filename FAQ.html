Frequently asked questions

What is Neko Pan?

Neko Pan is a cat-themed bakery that offers handcrafted pastries, breads, and sweets inspired by all things feline. We combine quality ingredients with adorable designs!

Are your products freshly baked?

Yes! All our treats are freshly baked daily to ensure the best taste and texture.

Do you offer delivery?

Yes, we offer delivery within select areas. You can check availability and place your orders through our website or social media pages.

Can I pre-order for events or bulk orders?

Absolutely! We accept pre-orders for birthdays, parties, and special events. Just contact us in advance so we can prepare everything purr-fectly.

Are your products vegetarian or vegan-friendly?

Many of our items are vegetarian. For vegan options, please check the product labels or ask our team—we're happy to accommodate when possible.

Do you have a physical store?

Yes! You can visit our cozy Neko Pan shop where you can enjoy pastries fresh out of the oven and snap pics with our cat-themed decor.

What makes Neko Pan special?

We're not just a bakery—we're a warm, whimsical space for cat lovers and dessert fans alike. Every pastry is made with love, care, and a touch of feline charm.
