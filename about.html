About us

Welcome to Neko Pan

Our Cakes Are Baked With a Whole Lot of Love (and a Dash of Whisker Magic)! At Nekopan, every treat is a paw-sonal creation made from scratch. From fluffy, feather-light to baking cakes from soft, fluffy loaves to dreamy, decadent cakes, we whip up thousands of sweet delights every day – each one bringing joy and smiles through its purrfect charm.

You might spot a Nekopan creation in your favorite café, bakery, or cozy little restaurant. Wherever our treats go, they bring a little warmth, a lot of flavor, and plenty of whiskers! We put our hearts, paws, and souls into finding the finest ingredients, pour our hearts into every bake, and stay devoted to delighting every single sweet tooth.

01

Why Choose us

At Neko Pan, we blend the warmth of homemade goodness with a cherie of feline fun. Each treat is made with love, quality ingredients, and a dash of whisker-worthy cuteness.

02

Mission

To bring joy through adorable, handcrafted cat-themed pastries that delight both the eyes and taste buds.

03

Vision

To become the purr-fect destination for cat-lovers and pastry fans around the world.
